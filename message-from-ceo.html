Message From CEO

Approximately 40% of medical services can be carried out via written digital or video consultation. This platform introduces a way of accessing high-profile professionals, anywhere in the world who, at predetermined costs, provide first and second medical opinions. The portal refers in particular to those who live in areas poorly served by medical resources of high consultative value, those who struggle to move and those who require the opinions of high-profile doctors in a short time.

Carlo Bulletti, MD
